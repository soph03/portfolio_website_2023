A little bit about me

Growing up, I found joy and interest in capturing memories on film, documenting travels through videography, and developing business ideas for non profits. Outside of work, you can find me drafting my next adventure, discovering new music, exploring culinary indulgences, or engaging in some form of physical activity. I'm currently learning about automation for facility management and obsessed with climbing, ANOTR radio @spotify, and waxy corn.
